Welcome to Diez Libras De Suciedad

At our Diez Libras De Suciedad, we strive to provide our customers with an authentic dining experience that captures the vibrant culture and flavours of Mexico. We take pride in using only the freshest ingredients in our dishes, and our talented chefs bring a wealth of culinary knowledge to the kitchen, ensuring that every dish is cooked to perfection. Our welcoming and attentive staff will make you feel right at home, and we're committed to providing excellent service to each and every one of our guests. Whether you're in the mood for traditional Tacos, Lobster Roll Sandwich, or a refreshing margarita, we've got you covered. Come and experience the taste of Mexico at our restaurant.

1975-1989

During this time period, our restaurant underwent a significant expansion, as we moved to a larger location in order to accommodate our growing customer base. We also added several new dishes to our menu, including our now-famous Lobster Pie Pizza and Crab and Street Corn Nachos. Our commitment to using fresh, high-quality ingredients remained unchanged, and we continued to take pride in providing exceptional service to our customers. With our expanded space and menu, we became a favourite dining destination for locals and tourists alike, and our reputation for authentic Mexican cuisine began to spread.

1990-2000

In the 1990s, our restaurant underwent another period of growth and innovation. We introduced several new menu items that were inspired by the cuisine of various regions in Mexico, and we also began to incorporate more vegetarian and gluten-free options into our offerings. Our commitment to quality and service never wavered, and we continued to be a go-to spot for those seeking delicious Mexican cuisine. We also began to cater more frequently to private events, and our catering services quickly became a popular choice for weddings, corporate events, and other special occasions.

2000-Now

In the 21st century, our restaurant has continued to evolve and grow. We've embraced technology by offering online ordering and delivery services, and we've also expanded our social media presence to connect with our customers in new and exciting ways. We've continued to innovate our menu, introducing new dishes and drinks that keep our customers coming back for more. And while we've grown and changed over the years, our commitment to using fresh, high-quality ingredients and providing exceptional service has remained constant. We're proud to be a part of the community and look forward to continuing to serve our loyal customers for many years to come.
